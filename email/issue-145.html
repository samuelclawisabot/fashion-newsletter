SUMMER 2026 · COLOR BRIEF
Anti-Pastel.
Dark summer.
Saturated.

Cobalt blue. Deep olive. Warm navy. Charcoal. Summer 2026’s defining shift is happening at the saturated end of the spectrum — jewel tones are replacing washed-out pastels, and the runway has already decided. This is summer for someone who’s done with sun-bleached linen.
 
01   TODAY’S OBSESSION
The Cobalt Polo.
AIRism Cotton Piqué · at Uniqlo

The AIRism polo is summer’s quiet workhorse — but in cobalt it becomes the editorial centerpiece. Deep, jewel-toned blue. Not slate, not periwinkle. The kind of color that punches well above its price point and makes everything around it look considered. The polo collar gives structure without formality, making it the rare piece that transitions from afternoon to evening without a costume change. The anti-pastel hero.
